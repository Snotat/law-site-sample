Services

Business Services
This covers all issues that comes up during business operations. Problems might arise during merging, opening new branches, relationship with competitors or host communities etc.
Criminal Services
Crimes are handled under this section. Suspects of murder, robbery, fraud and other crimes can have a lawyer to defend them against such prosecutions, as well as victims of such crimes.
Family Services
This covers matters relating to family relationships. Some families go through hard times and disagreements that requires a lawyer for dialogue, divorce, child custody, property sharing etc.
Consultancy and Dialogue Services
This covers scenerios where a client needs legal information and advice from a lawyer before making critical decisions/actions or to review documents such as agreements, leases, wills etc.
Mediation and Arbitration Services
Under this service, a lawyer can be hired to help disagreeing parties to negotiate a settlement that will satisfy all parties(mediator) or enforce a decision that must stand(arbitrator).
Document preparation
Legal documents can be drafted by anyone. However, it is not encouraged. A quailified lawyer should be hired to draft whatever documents that requires signature(s).
